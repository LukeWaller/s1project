Billabong

Billabong, is a comfortable beach wear clothing brand, and they usually have very cute items for about the same prices you would expect to pay at the mall. Every once in a while you can find some steals and some very cute beach dresses or swimsuits that are to die for, that you really cannot get anywhere else. If you are looking for comfy beach like clothing. Billabong is the place to look.

Dcshoes

No fuss purchase and return. I ordered a snowboarding jacket from this company which arrived in good time and was as described. I don't understand why DC shoes gets so many bad reviews.The jacket was beautiful and as described on their website.Unfortunately it didn't fit me well enough so I returned it. Yes, the postage to Belgium cost money but I don't have a big problem with this. At least their clothing is available for us to buy in the UK.You get to buy and try an item in the comfort of your own home all for £7-8. Frankly it is so cold right now, I prefer this to trekking out to a retail store in town. Also they processed the return within a few days no fuss and money was back in my account.I would buy again.
